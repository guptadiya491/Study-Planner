S.M.A.R.T Goals

Ensure your goals are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound to avoid aimless studying.

Micro-Goals

Break a huge exam into 10 smaller "mini-exams." Completing small pieces prevents burnout and overwhelm.

Reward Systems

Set a reward for hitting a goal. Positive reinforcement builds a stronger neural connection between effort and success.
