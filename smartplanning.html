Master Smart Planning 🧠

Success isn't about studying harder, it's about studying smarter.

The 80/20 Rule

Identify the 20% of the material that will likely account for 80% of your exam results. Focus your deepest energy there first.

Time Blocking

Don't just list tasks; assign them a specific time slot. This prevents "decision fatigue" when you sit down to work.

Active Recall

Instead of re-reading notes, test yourself. Planning sessions for self-quizzing is the most effective way to retain info.

Planned Breaks

Use the Pomodoro technique (25m study / 5m break). A planned break keeps your brain's "processing power" high.
